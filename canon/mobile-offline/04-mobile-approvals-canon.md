# CRM OS Mobile Approvals Canon

## Purpose

Mobile approvals allow managers to review urgent business decisions without desktop access.

## Supported Approval Types

- Quote discount approval
- Credit limit approval
- Order cancellation approval
- Purchase request approval
- Payment exception approval
- Workflow approval request

## Rules

- Approval details must show business impact.
- Reject/change-request requires reason.
- AI recommendation may assist but cannot approve automatically.
- Approval actions must respect RBAC and tenant boundaries.
- Every approval action must create audit and timeline events.
