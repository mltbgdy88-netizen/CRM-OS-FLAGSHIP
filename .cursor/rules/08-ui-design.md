# 08 UI Design Rules v8

**Scope:** Dedicated UI/UX sprint only. **Not** Sprint-04+ domain sprints — see `docs/DECISIONS.md` and `07-frontend.md`.

- CRM OS is dark-first, enterprise-grade, high-density and AI-native.
- Use design tokens; do not hardcode raw colors or one-off spacing.
- Tables, forms, cards, drawers and AI panels follow the documented UX system.
- AI suggestions must be clearly labelled and require approve, edit or reject where they affect data.
