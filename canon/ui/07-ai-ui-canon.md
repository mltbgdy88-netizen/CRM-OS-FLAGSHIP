# CRM OS AI UI Canon

## Principle

AI is an auditable assistant layer, not a hidden autonomous actor.

## Required AI Components

- AICopilotPanel
- AIQuickActions
- AIDomainInsight
- AIReviewControls
- AIConfidenceIndicator
- AIContextSourceList
- AIReplySuggestion
- AIFormSuggestion
- AITableActionReview
- AITimelineEvent

## AI Rules

- AI content must be visibly labeled.
- AI must show source context where relevant.
- AI must expose confidence/caution text.
- AI suggestions must require approve/edit/reject controls.
- AI must not silently create, update, delete, approve, send, assign, export, or mutate data.
- Applied AI actions must be visible in timeline/audit flows.
