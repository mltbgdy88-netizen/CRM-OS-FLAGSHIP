# CRM OS Responsive & Accessibility Canon

## Breakpoints

- Mobile: <768px
- Tablet: 768px–1023px
- Desktop: 1024px–1439px
- Wide: >=1440px

## Desktop

- Persistent sidebar and topbar.
- Docked right panel where useful.
- High-density tables.
- Multi-column dashboards.

## Tablet

- Collapsed sidebar or drawer.
- Right panel overlay.
- Split views stack when needed.

## Mobile

- Companion experience.
- Tasks, approvals, notifications, customer search, and quick actions first.
- Tables may become card/list views.

## Accessibility Rules

- Keyboard reachability required.
- Focus-visible required.
- Icon-only buttons require labels.
- Forms require labels and linked errors.
- Status cannot rely on color alone.
- Drawers and modals manage focus.
