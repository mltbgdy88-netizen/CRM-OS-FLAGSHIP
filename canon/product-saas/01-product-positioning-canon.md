# CRM OS Product Positioning Canon

## Product Category

CRM OS is not a generic CRM. It is a Customer Operating System combining:

- CRM
- Sales Pipeline
- Quote and Order Management
- Product Catalog and Inventory
- Finance Lite
- Omnichannel Inbox
- Support and Ticketing
- Workflow Automation
- B2B Dealer Portal
- AI Assistant
- API and Integration Hub
- SaaS Admin Panel

## North Star

CRM OS must feel like an operational command center for a business, not only a sales record system.

## Primary Promise

CRM OS helps teams manage the full customer lifecycle:

Lead → Customer → Opportunity → Quote → Order → Shipment → Invoice → Payment → Support → Retention

## Differentiation

- AI-native but auditable
- Multi-tenant from day one
- Sales + operations + finance-lite in one flow
- Strong B2B, dealer, branch, and inventory orientation
- Enterprise security and SaaS governance built into the core
