# CRM OS Prediction & Recommendation Canon

## Prediction Use Cases

- Lead conversion probability
- Opportunity win probability
- Customer churn risk
- Payment delay risk
- Stock shortage risk
- Ticket escalation risk
- Sales forecast
- Quote approval risk

## Recommendation Use Cases

- Next best action
- Suggested follow-up
- Suggested discount warning
- Suggested upsell/cross-sell
- Suggested owner assignment
- Suggested task creation
- Suggested ticket reply

## Required UX Controls

Each recommendation must include:

```text
Title
Summary
Reason
Source/context
Confidence/caution
Approve/Edit/Reject controls when actionable
```

## Audit Rule

Accepted recommendations must be traceable in timeline/audit logs.
