# CRM OS Analytics Intelligence Canon

## Purpose

Analytics Intelligence connects reports, dashboards, natural language questions, and AI summaries.

## Capabilities

- Dashboard insight generation
- Report explanation
- KPI anomaly explanation
- Forecast commentary
- Segment analysis
- Pipeline health analysis
- Revenue risk summary
- Support workload summary

## Rules

- Analytics must use permission-aware datasets.
- AI explanations must not fabricate numbers.
- Source metrics and filters must be visible.
- Export requires permission.
- Scheduled AI reports must be auditable.
